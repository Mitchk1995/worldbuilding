# Crossers

Status: working design, not canon.

- Crossing shape: double-life crossing.
- The player enters as themself, and real time matters.
- Crossers are rare but real.
- They do not come from the same universe. They arrive from across the multiverse.
- Ordinary life does not revolve around crossers.
- Some people and institutions quietly track rumors, traces, and patterns linked to crossers.
- Player-facing discovery should stay protected by keeping hidden truths in the background and deciding only surface reality openly.
