# Foundation Index

Status: working design, not canon.

## Files

- `premise.md`: genre spine, tone, and the world's central promise
  Use when the question is about overall feel, purpose, or what kind of story-world this is.
- `power-and-growth.md`: how people grow, awaken, and change through travel
  Use when the question is about power, training, awakening, or progression without game mechanics.
- `geography-and-expansion.md`: the center, routes, frontier layers, and long-horizon scale
  Use when the question is about map feel, travel space, frontier layers, or how the world can widen over time.
- `outer-island-belt.md`: the first outward belt of hard islands and widening passages beyond the threshold hub
  Use when the question is about what people imagine first beyond the hub, the early frontier's physical shape, or how the world widens without closing off its future scale.
- `threshold-hub.md`: the last safe route hub between the old center and the true frontier
  Use when the question is about departure culture, recoveries, rumor flow, or where center life starts to give way to frontier life.
- `society-and-expeditions.md`: the center's institutions, frontier culture, and rising outer powers
  Use when the question is about expeditions, legitimacy, recoveries, route culture, or political life between center and frontier.
- `peoples-and-intimacy.md`: species variety, social tendencies, romance, and slower life options
  Use when the question is about peoples, gender balance, attraction, social energy, romance, or non-frontier ways of living.
- `crossers.md`: what is publicly true about rare multiverse crossers
  Use when the question is about isekai crossing, real-time connection, or what the world knows about crossers.
- `unknowns.md`: what stays intentionally undefined for now
  Use when checking whether something should remain hidden, undecided, or out of scope for the current slice.
