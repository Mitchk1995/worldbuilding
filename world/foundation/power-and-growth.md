# Power And Growth

Status: working design, not canon.

- Power shape: one deeper reality underneath a few major traditions.
- Main traditions so far:
  - disciplined paths built through practice, travel, and lived experience
  - inner awakenings that emerge slowly under pressure and growth
  - place-bound shaping, where environments quietly influence what a person can become
- Growth should feel lived, social, physical, and reputational rather than like a visible game system.
- Real power takes time. Early life in the world stays vulnerable and earned.
- Travel matters because different places quietly shape skills, instincts, and possible awakenings.
- The farther world should not feel like a simple numeric difficulty ladder.
- Outer regions can be more demanding, more specialized, or simply more alien to central habits without always being uniformly harsher.
- The center is wrong about the limits of reality, but the setting should still feel grounded in discoverable truths rather than arbitrary reality warping.
