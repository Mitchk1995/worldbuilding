# Peoples And Intimacy

Status: working design, not canon.

- The world should include multiple races or species rather than only humans.
- Do not lock a giant species list yet. Add peoples slowly so the setting stays readable.
- Many major societies should skew female-dominated in power, social influence, and sexual initiative.
- The broad social energy should feel more intimacy-forward, bond-driven, and bonobo-like than dominance-first or chimp-like.
- That energy should create warmth, flirtation, romance, social play, and pressure, not just constant explicitness.
- Romance should be a real pillar of the world, not a side feature.
- Slower life paths should be valid too: companionship, local belonging, domestic life, and non-frontier futures should feel alive rather than like failure states.
- Different peoples should express gender, courtship, family, and attraction differently so the whole world does not flatten into one culture.
- The old center should carry more sticky ideology around status, propriety, and what counts as respectable life.
- Farther-out societies should often feel more truth-tested, more adaptive, and more open about sex, intimacy, and social roles than the center.
