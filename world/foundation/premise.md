# Premise

Status: working design, not canon.

- Genre spine: adventure first, built for a long-form isekai journey rather than a game-like progression loop.
- Writing feel: vivid, adventurous, emotionally sincere, lighter than grimdark, and capable of strong bonds, rivalries, and romance.
- Expedition premise: many kinds of people leave home for different reasons, but the age itself is defined by going outward to change your fate.
- The frontier's promise is power, freedom, and truth all at once.
- The great expedition age is happening now because older routes and traces from a lost age are reopening.
- Most people still orient themselves around an old central heartland, but that picture is becoming outdated.
- The world should be able to widen dramatically over time through discoveries that can eventually overturn politics, history, scale, and the meaning of power.
- First outer-world emotional impact: true frontier places should hit newcomers with other people's competence first, and with a disturbing sense of belonging soon after. Scale can widen more gradually behind that.
